About Us

A long history

Our story

Prior to the company’s inception in 2018, our founder Elie Nour made his mark in the industry in 2005 with Berkshire Securities, until it was acquired by Manulife Securities in 2007. His successful tenure at Manulife earned him the title Manulife’s top producer in 2011 and 2012. Since then, Elie Nour has been ranked in the "Top 50 Canada’s Best Advisors” by WP magazine for the past 9 consecutive years. Mr. Nour, often credits his success to hard work while always keeping in mind clients' goals. His disciplined and conservative investment style sets the tone for our firm as we manage our clients’ wealth to the utmost standard.

Team of Specialist
Our team consists of industry specialists and experts in various financial sectors. Our highly diverse team is ready to help you achieve long term success no matter the complexity of your financial situation.

Independence
Many investment advisors lack the freedom to provide clients with the most suitable advice due to being limited to proprietary products. Being independent, we are not bound by such restrictions which allows us to serve our clients better without any conflicts of interest.

Personalized Approach
By maintaining constant and consistent dialog with our clients we are able to bring to their attention the latest macro trends concerning interest rates, inflation outlook, and tax laws that impact the intricacies of their Wealth Plan.
